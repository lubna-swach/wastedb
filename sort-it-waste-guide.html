Why it matters

Sorting at home is the whole game

Once different kinds of waste get mixed and wet, almost none of it can be recovered. A few seconds of sorting at the source keeps materials usable, keeps waste pickers safe, and keeps your neighbourhood out of the landfill.

60%+

of our waste is wet

Food and garden waste can become compost or biogas — but only if it's never mixed with plastic or paper.

♻️

Mixed = wasted

A clean PET bottle is recyclable. The same bottle soaked in gravy is just landfill. Empty and dry decides its fate.

The people behind the pickup

SWaCH collects what you sort

SWaCH is a cooperative of waste pickers that collects door-to-door across Pune in partnership with the Municipal Corporation. When you segregate properly, their work is safer and far more of your waste actually gets recovered.

For dry recyclables, reusable goods and e-waste, many items can be picked up on request through the V-collect drive.
